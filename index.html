Implementation guardrails

  • Keep the model advisory: the complaint owner remains accountable for final assessment selection.
  • The supported technical assessment outputs are DeviceHistory Review, Mfg Assessment, Design Assessment, and CM/OEM Assessment.
  • Validate with historical complaints, measure missed required assessments, and require quality/regulatory sign-off before release.
  • Store rationale, matched facts, model/rule version, reviewer override, and audit trail for every recommendation.
