Implementation guardrails

  • Keep the model advisory: the complaint owner remains accountable for final assessment selection.
  • The only supported output for now is whether DeviceHistory Review is needed.
  • Validate with historical complaints, measure missed required DHR reviews, and require quality/regulatory sign-off before release.
  • Store rationale, matched facts, rule version, reviewer override, and audit trail for every recommendation.
